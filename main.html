Shahid Afridi #hopenotout

Shahid Khan Afridi, Cricketer, philanthropist, extraordinaire. This man requires little introduction. Former captain of the Pakistan national cricket team, well known for his amazing performance in the cricket field he has touched the hearts of many Pakistanis. Affectionately known nation-wide as ‘boom-boom’, Shahid Afridi, through his philanthropic work has shown that he is a true captain both on the cricket field and off of it.
