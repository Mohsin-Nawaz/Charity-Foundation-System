Health

Sahibzada Fazal
Rehman Charity
Hospital

The hospital is equipped with all major machinery and facilities, complete with 16 beds, the hospital is providing free primary healthcare facilities to the poor and ailing community since the hospital started its operations in March 2014.

Health

Thar hospital

SAF has recently collaborated with Sindh Engro Coal Mining Co. (a subsidiary of Engro Power Gen) and Indus Hospital to build a 250 Bed State-of-the-Art Hospital in Islamkot area of Thar region of Sindh. SAF being focused on the health of mother & child, the construction cost of mother & child block will be funded by SAF and it will be named as Shahid Afridi Foundation Mother & Child Block.

Education

SAF Schools
Initiative

SAF aims to nourish the future of the children of Pakistan. SAF will be focusing on education initiatives whereby we will provide free of cost education to the most vulnerable children across Pakistan and has initiated the first school in Malikpur area of Punjab.

Education

Providing Infrastructure

The Foundation decided to put its expertise to good use by aiding Karachi University in completely revamping their archaic infrastructure and providing them with state-of-the-art facilities.

Clean Water

Tirah Valley:
Supplying Water

SAF is now currently working on different initiatives in Tirah Maidan, Khyber Agency, FATA including water shortages, education, sports & rehabilitation. The current focus is on WASH projects whereby addressing water issues in the area by providing water storage through the construction of water tanks and by connecting them through pipelines from the water deposits.

Clean Water

Hand pumps across
Pakistan

Pakistan is among the top 10 countries of the world with the greatest number of people living without access to safe water. With as many as 16 million people in the country have no choice but to collect unsafe water from unsafe sources, SAF recognizes the importance of dealing with this situation as efficiently as possible one marginalized community at a time.
