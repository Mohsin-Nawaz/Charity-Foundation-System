The History

Shahid Afridi Foundation, founded in March 2014, was a step towards a better Pakistan by the famous cricketer Shahid Afridi also known as Boom Boom worldwide. The organization was established with the purpose of improving the living conditions of the poor and destitute in the society.
The first project of the foundation was successful development of a hospital in Tangi Banda Village of Kohat with the name of “Sahibzada Fazal Rehman Memorial and Charity Hospital”. The foundation also worked on the water shortage issues in the villages of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by fixing simple hand pumps. It also gave scholarships to 12 deserving students of Farabi Government School in year 2014, and shortlists candidate each year to proceed with the tradition. SAF also displayed support with Dar-ul-Sukun (Karachi) by adopting the special children ward on 16th October 2015.

The Vision & Mission

Here, at the Shahid Afridi Foundation (SAF) we aspire to truly be of service to our country through helping those communities who have been ignored for so long. However, we recognize the magnitude of the task we hope to achieve and realize that it is a useless pursuit without the help of our donors and followers.
The primary objective is to establish a healthy, stable and secure life for the underprivileged people of the country, especially children. We aspire to transform the lives of our people by generating rays of hopes through our services and programs.
Similar to other Non-Profit organizations, Shahid Afridi Foundation (SAF) also has a simple vision to help the world enjoy peace and equality through the improvement of health and education.
SAF aims to go global and create alliance with other reputable foundations to serve people irrespective of any race, religion and origin.

Shahid Afridi Foundation (SAF) is committed to changing the lives of people however whatever we do would be incomplete without your love and support. The foundation works like a family, a family dedicated to providing better healthcare, education and clean drinking water to every member of society and become a ray of hope for children, youngsters and adults alike.
